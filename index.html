ABOUT ME

Hello there! I'm a freelance front-end web developer based in Kansas City. My goal is to bring your web projects to life and deliver an exceptional online experience. Whether you need a robust database-driven web app or a sleek and responsive static site, I've got you covered.
I specialize in crafting outstanding solutions using a wide range of technologies. My expertise includes HTML5/CSS3, JavaScript, React, JQuery, PHP, SQL, and Python. Whether you have a substantial budget or are working with limited resources, I can tailor a solution that suits your needs perfectly. So, if you're looking for a dedicated web developer who is passionate about creating impactful digital experiences, I'm here to help you every step of the way. Let's collaborate and turn your vision into reality!

Below, you can explore a collection of my previous work, showcasing the diversity of projects I've undertaken. From ambitious dynamic apps to elegant static sites, each project demonstrates the meticulous attention to detail and craftsmanship that goes into my work.
